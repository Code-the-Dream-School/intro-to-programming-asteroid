About

    Experience

Before that role, I was employed by the Chapel Hill- Carrboro school system, Chapel Hill Parks & Rec department, and RSI, inc. where I was thrived with increasingly challenging responsibilities. I have produced, directed, and coordinated educational and recreational programming for K-12 students as well as adults with development disabilities.
